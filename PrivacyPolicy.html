Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fansfans ("we," "us," or "our") handles information for the mobile game Magic Liquid Sort ("the Game").

2. Data Collection and AdMob

To support the development and maintenance of this free game, we use Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. Google AdMob may collect and process certain data from your device for the purpose of serving personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

The data collected may include, but is not limited to, device identifiers (such as the Android advertising ID), IP address, and app usage information. This data is handled in accordance with the Google AdMob and Google Play Developer Policies.

For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. Local Game Data

The Game may store certain data locally on your device to enable core gameplay functionality. This includes:

  • Your game progress (e.g., unlocked levels).
  • Game settings (e.g., sound preferences).

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. Deleting the game from your device will also delete all of this local data.

4. No Other Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob, Magic Liquid Sort does not use any other third-party analytics, data collection, or social media services.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
